## New Gem

Yesterday, I pushed a gem to RubyGems called PrettyMagic. It takes MTG card mana codes and symbols and converts them to HTML
that the Mana and Keyrune fonts can use. It takes codes formatted the way Scryfall does it and outputs the HTML.

Check it out on github @ https://github.com/danwiseman/pretty_magic
